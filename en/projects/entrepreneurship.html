APEC Forum on Digital Innovation and Youth Entrepreneurship

Visit Project Site

Over the years, the government has been promoting innovation and entrepreneurship policies to cultivate talents. Various programs are initiated for universities to establish innovation and entrepreneurship friendly campus environments through enhancing course quality, consolidating industry-academia resources, and fostering talents with an entrepreneurial spirit. The forum echoes APEC’s 2019 priorities: digital economy and the service industry, regional connectivity, and growth in women’s roles. As themes of the year are aligned with efforts to facilitate regional youth entrepreneurship and innovation in SMEs, Taiwan will continue to seek support from other economies, promote our innovative and entrepreneurial capacity, and further increase Taiwan’s visibility in the global community.
