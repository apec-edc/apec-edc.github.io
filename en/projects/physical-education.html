APEC Sports Policy Network (ASPN)

In 2016, the Ministry of Education (MOE) joined efforts with the APEC Human Resources Development Working Group (HRDWG) for the first time. Under the theme ‘APEC Education Training and Career Planning for Young Athletes’, the MOE hosted the 2016 APEC High Level Policy Dialogue on Education and Career Planning for Young Athletes and established the APEC Sports Policy Network (ASPN).

Launched in 2017, the ASPN 123 Mode stands for two international ASPN meetings held and three e-newspapers published annually. It creates a dialogue for APEC members, experts and scholars to share in this regard on a regular basis.

Two international seminars in 2018 were held in Taipei and Tokyo respectively. Themes of the meetings were Career Development of Sport Talents in Digital Age, and the Development of Sports Professionals for Mega Games.
