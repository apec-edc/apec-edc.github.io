MOE APEC Scholarship

In order to strengthen cross-border education cooperation and exchange with APEC members and promote global mobility for the youth, the Ministry of Education has set up the “Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation Scholarship of the Ministry of Education” since 105, providing 10 places each year to APEC (Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation, Asia Pacific). The "Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation (APEC) Scholarship of the Ministry of Education provides areas for short-term research, including Taiwan's humanities and arts, education and culture, social sciences, natural sciences and related comparative research. Awards and related rights and obligations include: The monthly subsidy for doctoral students is NT$25,000, and the post-doctoral research fee is NT$40,000. It also provides one ticket for the round-trip economy class of the most direct route from the place of residence to Taiwan.
