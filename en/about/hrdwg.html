About Human Resources Development Working Group (HRDWG)

HRDWG’s Establishment

Under the structure of APEC, HRD, among the 11 working groups, is founded in 1990. The primary goal is to share knowledge, experience and skills to enhance human resource development and encourage sustainable economic growth.

HRDWG’s Missions

HRD aims to improve people’s well-being by promoting economic growth and development, and also to play a vital part in achieving free, open trade and investment according to the commitment of the Bogor Declaration. The goal might be achieved through the following ways:

  • Developing knowledge and skills in the 21st century;
  • Connecting HRD to the global economy;
  • Responding to social issues caused by globalization.

The Structure of HRDWG

In HRDWG, The Human Resources Development, NDC is responsible for overall coordination. It is also in charge of contacting LSPN. The Workforce Development Agency of MOL and the Department of International and Cross-Strait Education of MOE is in charge of CBN and EDNET respectively.

The roles of the three subgroups under HRDWG are defined as follows:

  • LSPN aims to enhance people’s capabilities and social integration and to create a healthier and more flexible labor market. It is expected that the working conditions and practices in the workplace will be improved and a stronger social safety net created by formulating useful information and policies regarding the labor market.
  • CBN aims to enhance people’s capabilities and strengthen the market by improving the manufacturing process, enterprise productivity and adaptability, the development of management and skills and the business control in the public, private and voluntary sectors.
  • EDNET aims to build a powerful, active learning system to improve education for all so that education will have a stronger influence on social, individual and economic development.

APEC is one of the few international organizations that Taiwan can participate as an official member. Therefore, MOE expects to share our educational features with others through the platform of APEC. By actively participating in APEC, we shall be able to create a positive international image, increase the communication with many experienced official representatives and to connect our own priority areas to such an important regional organization as APEC.
