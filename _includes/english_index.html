About APEC

APEC was established in 1989 with the objective of promoting trade, investment and cooperation in the Asia-Pacific. The ultimate objective of course is to achieve prosperity for the region. APEC was the brainchild of former Australian Prime Minister Bob Hawke. It first met in Canberra in 1989 with 12 members. Chinese Taipei joined in 1991. There are currently 21 member economics.
